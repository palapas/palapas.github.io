About Us

SEABREEZE PALAPA

A company dedicated to making your paradise come true. We have 15 years of experience and proudly serve the surrounding Houston areas. Seabreeze Palapas specializes in Tiki Bars, Decks, Palapas, Cedar, Furniture, Synthetic Thatch & Much More. We take your dreams and bring them to life. We partner up with you and walk you through the Palapa construction process, step by step. From the beginning of the construction to the completion of your project.

PALAPA DESIGN

Our designs are all custom built on the job site by the most advanced and professional Palapa builders in the Houston Area. We are committed to giving our customers the highest quality of workmanship and materials, in a timely manner, with competitive pricing. Each Palapa is a hands-on job in which we follow through from preconstruction to completion. For the structure of our Palapas (the poles and framework) we use only the highest quality Cedar for its longevity and durability. Our competitors use pine, which retains water and rots quickly. Our leaves are individually secured into place to maintain resistance from any windstorm or hurricane.

OUR GUARANTEE

Whether you're improving your residential or commercial property, it's important to know that your hard-earned money is being spent wisely. We offer the security of a 5, 15, and 20 year product warranty, as well as a team that's committed to excellent work. All natural thatch palapas with a net installed come with a 5 year warranty.
